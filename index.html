How to join us

  • Write to VP Membership at vpm@odense.toastmasters.dk
  • Fill out the membership form that you will receive from them and pay.
  • Start taking roles and grow as a speaker!

Guest attendance: Free.

Membership: as a member you choose a pathway and can start doing speeches, do all the meeting roles, join the board etc.

Pathways and Basecamp

Toastmasters International, the international umbrella for all Toastmasters clubs, provides a vast amount of learning material on their learning platform Basecamp.

As a member you get access to this platform and it serves as an interactive tutor for your progress through Pathways.

If you have any questions about Pathways or Basecamp, ask the VP Education (email: vpe@odense.toastmasters.dk).

You can access it here: Basecamp (Pathways).
